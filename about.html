About Us

STEEL@MAJOR was founded by a skilled welder and fabricator dedicated to bringing high-quality, custom steelwork to local homeowners and builders. It is a small local business that specializes in manufacturing and installing high-quality steel products such as gates, burglar bars, and fences. Customers can choose from a range of in-house designs or submit their own.

We pride ourselves on quality, durability, and personalized service.
